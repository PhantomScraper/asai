<h1>Demo capabilities of the Kit</h1>
<p>The kit offers a platform for demonstrating and evaluating the capabilities of Ultra-Wideband technology in various scenarios.</p>
<hr class="docutils">
<div class="section" id="nearby-interaction-demo-with-an-iphone">
<h2>Nearby Interaction demo with an iPhone</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>Prepare for setup</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">An iPhone device with U1 chip compatibility.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Qorvo Nearby Interaction application installed from the AppStore.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">One or more Nodes (white device) configured in Nearby Interaction mode.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm sd-text-center custom-card docutils">

</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-mb-3 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>Demonstration</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Demonstrates Nearby Interaction and FiRa compatibility with a smartphone.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Accurate distance and angle measurement, displaying precise direction to the Node within the smartphone application.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Typical applications: find my things, follow me, smart remote control, access control.</p></li>
</ul>

<h2>Locate Device demo using Angle-of-Arrival (AoA)</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">

<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>Prepare for setup</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">A desktop with Qorvo UWB Ranging &amp; AoA Demo App installed.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">One Node with an AoA antenna (gray device) and one or more Nodes without an AoA antenna (white device) configured in UCI mode.</p></li>

</ul>

</div>
</div>

<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Demonstrates FiRa compatibility between devices.</p></li>

<li><p class="sd-card-text">Typical applications: Access control, follow me, locate and track objects or devices within an indoor environment.</p></li>

<div class="section" id="infrastructure-less-proximity-demo">
<h2>Infrastructure-less proximity Demo</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>Prepare for setup</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Two or more Nodes configured as Tags.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm sd-text-center custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<a class="reference internal image-reference" href="../../../_images/infrastructure-less_proximity_demo.png"><img alt="../../../_images/infrastructure-less_proximity_demo.png" src="/docs-assets/_images/infrastructure-less_proximity_demo.png" style="width: 288.0px; height: 327.84000000000003px;"></a>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-mb-3 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>Demonstration</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Demonstrates infrastructure-less proximity between the Nodes.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">TWR distance measurement between a Node and all its surrounding nodes.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Trigger alarm using LED, haptic motor or buzzer when Nodes are in close proximity. The distance threshold is configurable.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Typical applications: Collision avoidance, social distancing, swarm coordination.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="section" id="downlink-tdoa-rtls-demo">
<h2>Downlink TDoA RTLS Demo</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>Prepare for setup</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Four Nodes configured as Anchors with fixed positions, and one or more Tag nodes configured as the DL-TDoA mode.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">An Android device with LEAPS Manager application installed and/or a terminal application on the desktop to display the location.</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm sd-text-center custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<a class="reference internal image-reference" href="../../../_images/downlink_tdoa_rtls_demo.png"><img alt="../../../_images/downlink_tdoa_rtls_demo.png" src="/docs-assets/_images/downlink_tdoa_rtls_demo.png" style="width: 252.00000000000003px; height: 286.86px;"></a>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-mb-3 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>Demonstration</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Demonstrates real-time navigation of an unlimited amount of Tags in receive-only mode with complete privacy using DL-TDoA technology.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Demonstrates the LEAPS Manager application with Tag position navigation.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Typical applications: Indoor navigation with mapping, autonomous robot and vehicle navigation, asset tracking with location data being sent through another communication channel.</p></li>
</ul>

<h2>TWR RTLS and Data Telemetry Demo</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">

<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Four Nodes configured as Anchors with fixed position, and one or more nodes configured as TWR and/or DL-TDoA Tag.</p></li>

<li><p class="sd-card-text">Demonstrates real-time navigation of an unlimited amount of Tags using DL-TDoA technology.</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">Typical applications: Indoor navigation, asset tracking and real-time data telemetry supporting uplink and downlink.</p></li>
