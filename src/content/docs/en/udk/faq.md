<p class="sd-card-text">The UDK (Ultra-Wideband Demo Kit) is an all-in-one demonstration ready-to-use kit. The kit provides a wide range of evaluation demonstrations of the Ultra-Wideband technology ranging from Nearby Interaction demo with an iPhone, FiRa compatible Two-Way Ranging with Angle-of-Arrival capabilities, infrastructure-less proximity between the tags, advanced navigation and tracking RTLS (Real-Time Location System) demonstrations using DL-TDoA (Downlink TDoA), UL-TDoA (Uplink TDoA) and TWR (Two-Way Ranging) techniques. Uplink and downlink data telemetry are also a part of the RTLS demo.</p>
