<span id="anchor-ni-app"></span><h1>Qorvo Nearby Interaction</h1>
<p>This section provides all the necessary installation and setup steps for <a class="reference external" href="https://apps.apple.com/tr/app/qorvo-nearby-interaction/id1615369084">Qorvo Nearby Interaction</a> application.
It requires iOS 16 for iPhones equipped with a U1 chip (model: iPhone 11, iPhone 12 &amp; iPhone 13).</p>
<div class="section" id="installation-instructions">
<h2>Installation instructions</h2>
<p>Nearby Interaction featuring background mode with third-party accessories was introduced starting from iOS 16.  It is required to update an iPhone to the iOS 16 version or later.
The <a class="reference external" href="https://apps.apple.com/tr/app/qorvo-nearby-interaction/id1615369084">Qorvo Nearby Interaction</a> iOS App is available for download on the Apple App Store:</p>
