<span id="id1"></span><h1>PANS PRO RTLS</h1>
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-3 sd-shadow-sm docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><span class="red-text-bold">PANS PRO is available for production. Contact us:</span> <a class="reference external" href="mailto:sales%40leaps.global">sales<span>@</span>leaps<span>.</span>global</a></p>
</div>
</div>
<p><a class="reference internal" href="#pans-pro-rtls"><span class="std std-ref">PANS PRO RTLS</span></a>, which is the Production version of PANS RTLS, developed by <a class="reference external" href="https://www.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS</a> for <a class="reference external" href="https://www.qorvo.com/">Qorvo</a> (former Decawave). It is a mature and improved technology that provides precise and accurate tracking of assets, people and equipments in real-time. <a class="reference internal" href="#pans-pro-rtls"><span class="std std-ref">PANS PRO RTLS</span></a> is using the TWR (Two-Way Ranging) method to locate tags around a network of fixed anchors, either via gateways connected to server, or directly on the asset or vehicle tracked, via APIs.</p>
