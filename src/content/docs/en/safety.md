<span id="id1"></span><h1>Safety</h1>
<p><span class="red-text-bold">To avoid malfunction or damage of the products and to protect the users, please pay attention to the following safety instructions</span></p>
<p><strong>Common Products</strong></p>
<ol class="arabic simple">
<li><p>Keep the device away from water or moisture to prevent damage to internal components.</p></li>
<li><p>Avoid exposing the device to extreme temperatures, both hot and cold, as it may affect its performance and longevity.</p></li>
<li><p>Handle the device with care to prevent physical damage, such as dropping or banging it against hard surfaces.</p></li>
<li><p>Ensure that the device is used in accordance with the specifications to prevent malfunctions or accidents.</p></li>
<li><p>Do not attempt to disassemble or modify the device yourself, as this may void the warranty and pose safety risks.</p></li>
<li><p>Use the device in well-ventilated areas to prevent overheating and ensure proper airflow.</p></li>
<li><p>Keep the device away from electromagnetic interference sources, such as other electronic devices or power lines, to avoid signal disruption.</p></li>
<li><p>If using the device in proximity to other people, ensure that they are aware of its operation and potential hazards.</p></li>
<li><p>Regularly inspect the device for any signs of wear or damage, and discontinue use if any issues are detected.</p></li>
<li><p>Follow proper storage procedures when the device is not in use, such as keeping it in a dry and dust-free environment.</p></li>
</ol>
<div class="admonition note">
<p class="admonition-title">Note</p>
<p>Acceptable range of temperature: For specific conditions such as charging, battery usage, etc., please refer to the technical specifications provided for each product.</p>
</div>
