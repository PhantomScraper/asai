<span id="id1"></span><h1>DWM3001C</h1>
<div class="line-block">
<div class="line">The Qorvo® DWM3001C is a fully integrated UWB transceiver module based on the Qorvo DW3110 IC. Integration of the DW3110 IC, nRF52833 MCU, planar UWB antenna, accelerometer, power management and crystal simplify the design cycle. The RF design is fully validated, tested, and calibrated. Low power consumption allows powering from batteries for extended periods, providing cost-effective implementation of UWB solutions.</div>
<div class="line">The DWM3001C can be used in two-way ranging and TDoA applications.</div>
<div class="line">The DWM3001C is designed to be compliant to the FiRa™ PHY and MAC specifications enabling interoperability with other FiRa™ compliant devices.</div>
</div>
<div class="admonition note">
<p class="admonition-title">Note</p>
<p>To get more information, please visit the <a class="reference external" href="https://www.qorvo.com/products/p/DWM3001C">Qorvo’s DWM3001C page</a>.</p>
</div>
<hr class="docutils">
<div class="section" id="key-features">
<h2>Key Features</h2>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p>IEEE 802.15.4-2015 and IEEE 802.15.4z BPRF compliant</p></li>
<li><p>Fully aligned with FiRa™ PHY, MAC and certification development</p></li>
<li><p>Supports Channels 5 (6.5 GHz) and 9 (8 GHz)</p></li>
<li><p>FCC, SRRC and ETSI certification (planned)</p></li>
<li><p>Nordic nRF52833 SoC with BLE transceiver</p></li>
<li><p>Supply voltage VCC: 2.5 V - 3.6 V</p></li>
<li><p>Bluetooth chip antenna</p></li>
<li><p>AES 128/256 Security block</p></li>
<li><p>Fully coherent receiver for maximum range and accuracy</p></li>
<li><p>Power consumption optimized for battery applications</p></li>
<li><p>Data rates of 850 kbps, 6.8 Mbps</p></li>
<li><p>Maximum packet length of 1023 bytes for high data throughput applications</p></li>
<li><p>Integrated MAC support features</p></li>
<li><p>Backward compatible with the DWM1001C module</p></li>
<li><p>Temperature range -40°C to +85°C</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="section" id="software-compatibility">
<h2>Software Compatibility</h2>
