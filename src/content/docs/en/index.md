<span id="welcome"></span><h1>Welcome to the LEAPS documentation!</h1>
<p>Welcome to the official documentation of <a class="reference external" href="https://www.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS</a>. Here, you will find comprehensive and detailed descriptions for every component of the <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/udk#udk-demo-kit"><span class="std std-ref">UDK DEMO KIT</span></a>, <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/leaps-rtls#leaps-rtls"><span class="std std-ref">LEAPS RTLS</span></a>, and <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/pans-pro-rtls#pans-pro-rtls"><span class="std std-ref">PANS PRO RTLS</span></a> (Real-Time Location System). Our documentation serves as a valuable resource, providing you with the necessary information to understand and utilize our system effectively.</p>
<p>In addition to the documentation, we are pleased to offer the <a class="reference external" href="https://forum.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS Forum</a>, a dedicated platform for our global community of customers and users. This forum allows you to engage in discussions, raise inquiries, and dive deeper into the system’s intricacies. It is a collaborative environment where you can connect with fellow users and benefit from shared knowledge and experiences.</p>
<p>We encourage you to explore our documentation and actively participate in the <a class="reference external" href="https://forum.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS Forum</a> to maximize the potential of our system.</p>

<div class="section" id="organization-of-the-documentation">
<h2>Organization of the documentation</h2>
<p>We organize our documentation into six sections:</p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p>The <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/leaps-solutions#leaps-solution"><span class="std std-ref">LEAPS SOLUTIONS</span></a> section provides concise information regarding the products and Ultra-Wideband Real-Time Location System offered by LEAPS.</p></li>
<li><p>The <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/udk#udk-demo-kit"><span class="std std-ref">UDK DEMO KIT</span></a> section offers comprehensive details and facilitates an easier start with an Ultra-Wideband all-in-one demonstration ready-to-use kit.</p></li>
<li><p>The <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/leaps-rtls#leaps-rtls"><span class="std std-ref">LEAPS RTLS</span></a> section explores an in-depth understanding of the advanced Ultra-Wideband navigation and tracking system LEAPS RTLS (Real-Time Location System).</p></li>
<li><p>The <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/pans-pro-rtls#pans-pro-rtls"><span class="std std-ref">PANS PRO RTLS</span></a> section explores an in-depth understanding of the legacy Ultra-Wideband navigation and tracking system PANS PRO RTLS (Real-Time Location System).</p></li>
<li><p>The <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/faq#common-faq"><span class="std std-ref">FAQ</span></a> section provides answers to frequently asked question (FAQs).</p></li>
<li><p>The <a class="reference internal" href="/docs/en/support#technical-support"><span class="std std-ref">SUPPORTS</span></a> section serves as a valuable resource and provides technical support information to ensure that assistance and guidance are readily available.</p></li>
</ul>

<div class="section" id="about-us">
<h2>About Us</h2>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p><a class="reference external" href="https://www.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS</a> is a company based in Prague (the Czech Republic, EU) that focuses on building and marketing Real-Time Location and Real-Time Telemetry systems based on Ultra-wideband.</p></li>
<li><p>With more than 10 years of experience in system development using Ultra-Wideband, LEAPS has one of the most innovative teams in converting the Ultra-wideband chip into a useful solution.</p></li>
<li><p>Our team consists of all senior embedded engineers who work with great passion from Prague, Ho Chi Minh City in Vietnam, and Pelotas in Brazil.</p></li>
<li><p><a class="reference external" href="https://www.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS</a> is the creator of the popular DWM1001C and PANS RTLS.</p></li>
<li><p><a class="reference external" href="https://www.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS</a> has been focusing on providing RTLS hardware and software, design services, and technology licensing.</p></li>
<li><p><a class="reference external" href="https://www.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS</a> RTLS is a collection of advanced software that provides location services for a wide range of use cases. It offers the most advanced fully-embedded UWB Sub-System on the market that covers features ranging from proximity, navigation and tracking to real-time data telemetry and routing backhaul. Imagine a GPS module but using Ultra-wideband and with far more functionality.</p></li>
<li><p><a class="reference external" href="https://www.leapslabs.com/">LEAPS</a> provides a new dimension that helps businesses locate new values, improve process efficiency, increase safety, improve reliability, navigate indoors, process automatization …</p></li>
</ul>
