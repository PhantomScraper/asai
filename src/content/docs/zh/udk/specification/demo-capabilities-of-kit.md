<h1>演示套件的功能</h1>
<p>该套件提供了一个平台，用于演示和评估 Ultra-Wideband 技术在各种场景下的能力。</p>
<hr class="docutils">
<div class="section" id="nearby-interaction-demo-with-an-iphone">
<h2>附近的iPhone交互演示</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>准备安装</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">兼容 U1 芯片的 iPhone 设备。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">从 AppStore 安装了 Qorvo Nearby Interaction 应用程序。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">一个或多个节点（白色设备）配置为附近交互模式。</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>

</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>演示</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">演示与智能手机的附近交互和 FiRa 兼容性。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">精确测量距离和角度，在智能手机应用中显示节点的精确方向。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">典型应用：寻找我的物品, 跟随我, 智能遥控, 门禁控制。</p></li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="section" id="locate-device-demo-using-angle-of-arrival-aoa">
<h2>使用到达角(AoA)定位设备演示</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>准备安装</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">安装了 Qorvo UWB Ranging &amp; AoA Demo App 的桌面。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">以 UCI 模式配置的一个带 AoA 天线的节点（灰色设备）和一个或多个不带 AoA 天线的节点（白色设备）。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">两根或更多根 USB-C 数据线，用于连接节点和 PC。</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm sd-text-center custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<a class="reference internal image-reference" href="../../../_images/locate_device_using_angle-of-arrival_demo.png"><img alt="../../../_images/locate_device_using_angle-of-arrival_demo.png" src="/docs-assets/zh-cn/_images/locate_device_using_angle-of-arrival_demo.png" style="width: 252.00000000000003px; height: 286.86px;"></a>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>演示</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">展示设备之间的 FiRa 兼容性。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">距离和角度测量，在桌面应用程序中显示启动器和应答器设备之间的方向。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">典型应用: 门禁控制, 跟踪, 定位和追踪室内环境中的物体或设备。</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<div class="section" id="infrastructure-less-proximity-demo">
<h2>无基础设施近距离演示</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>准备安装</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">配置为标记的两个或多个节点。</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm sd-text-center custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<a class="reference internal image-reference" href="../../../_images/infrastructure-less_proximity_demo.png"><img alt="../../../_images/infrastructure-less_proximity_demo.png" src="/docs-assets/zh-cn/_images/infrastructure-less_proximity_demo.png" style="width: 288.0px; height: 327.84000000000003px;"></a>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-mb-3 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>演示</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">展示节点之间无基础设施的接近性。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">节点与其周围所有节点之间的TWR距离测量。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">当节点非常接近时，使用LED, 触觉马达或蜂鸣器触发警报。 距离阈值是可配置的。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">典型应用： 避免碰撞, 社交距离, 蜂群协调。</p></li>
</ul>

<div class="section" id="downlink-tdoa-rtls-demo">
<h2>下行链路TDoA RTLS演示</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">

<li><p class="sd-card-text">四个节点配置为固定位置的锚点，一个或多个标签节点配置为下行链路TDoA模式。</p></li>

<li><p class="sd-card-text">典型应用： 带有地图的室内导航, 自主机器人和车辆导航, 通过其它通信渠道发送位置数据的资产追踪。</p></li>

<div class="section" id="twr-rtls-and-data-telemetry-demo">
<h2>TWR RTLS 和数据遥测演示</h2>
<div class="sd-container-fluid sd-sphinx-override sd-mb-4 docutils">
<div class="sd-row sd-row-cols-2 sd-row-cols-xs-2 sd-row-cols-sm-2 sd-row-cols-md-2 sd-row-cols-lg-2 docutils">
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>准备安装</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">四个节点配置为具有固定位置的锚点，以及一个或多个节点配置为TWR和/或 DL-TDoA标签。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">安装了LEAPS Manager应用程序的Android设备，和/或桌面上显示位置的终端应用程序。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">安装了LEAPS Server软件的桌面，用于定位, 数据采集和可视化。</p></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sd-col sd-d-flex-row sd-m-0 docutils">
<div class="sd-card sd-sphinx-override sd-w-100 sd-shadow-sm custom-card docutils">
<div class="sd-card-body docutils">
<p class="sd-card-text"><strong>演示</strong></p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><p class="sd-card-text">展示实时导航, 追踪，以及使用TWR技术进行数据遥测的两种方式。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">演示利用DL-TDoA技术实时导航无限量的标签。</p></li>
<li><p class="sd-card-text">典型应用：室内导航, 资产跟踪和支持上行链路和下行链路的实时数据遥测。</p></li>
